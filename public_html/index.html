About

DIT is devoted to excellence in teaching, learning, and research, and to developing leaders in many disciplines who make a difference globally. DIT faculty are engaged with teaching and research to push the boundaries of human knowledge. The University has twelve degree-granting Schools in addition to the DIT Institute for Advanced Study.

Established in 1978, DIT is one of the youngest institution of higher education in the Ireland. The University, which is based in Dublin, Ireland, has an enrollment of over 20,000 degree candidates, including undergraduate, graduate, and professional students. DIT has more than 370,000 alumni around the world.
